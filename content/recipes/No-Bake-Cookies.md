title: No Bake Cookies
authorName: Tonja Cone
lang: en-US
category: Desserts
ingredients:
 - 2 C sugar
 - 1 cube butter
 - 4 tsp cocoa
 - 1/2 C milk
 - 1 tsp vanilla
 - 3 C oatmeal
 - 1/2 C chopped nuts
 - 1 C coconut

directions:
  - Mix first four ingredients in large saucepan over medium heat.
  - Add remaining ingredients.
  - Drop spoonfuls onto waxed paper.
