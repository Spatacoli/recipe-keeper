title: Turdees or Winoes
authorName: Fern Spatafore
lang: en-US
category: Desserts
ingredients:
 - 11 C flour
 - 14 eggs
 - 2 C sugar
 - 1 1/3 C white wine
 - 1/2 C vegetable oil
 - 2 tsp salt
 - 1 C honey

directions:
  - Mix until stiff enough to handle.
  - Shape, fry in 1 qt hot vegetable oil until brown.
  - Let cool.
  - Bring 1 qt honey and 1 cup sugar to boil.
  - Dip turdees in this mixture.
